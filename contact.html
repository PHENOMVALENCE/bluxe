Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekend events.
Do you provide hookah services for outdoor events?
Yes, we provide hookah services for both indoor and outdoor events. For outdoor events, we may need to make special arrangements depending on weather conditions.
What is included in the setup?
Our setup includes the hookah(s), hoses, coal, tobacco flavors, and all necessary accessories. We also handle the setup, maintenance during the event, and cleanup afterward.
Can I customize my flavor selection?
We offer a wide range of premium flavors. You can specify your preferences when booking, or we can recommend popular options based on your event type.
